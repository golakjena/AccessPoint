Access Point Financial, Inc. Recognized By Association for Corporate Growth

June 30, 2015

Atlanta-based hotel lender named ACG Atlanta Georgia Fast 40 honoree

Access Point Financial, Inc. (APF), a direct full-service lender and capital markets enterprise focused on the hospitality industry, has been recognized by the Association for Corporate Growth® (ACG) Atlanta as an ACG Atlanta Georgia Fast 40 honoree. The annual event occurs each spring, and honors the fastest growing companies headquartered in the state of Georgia. APF was ranked the 6th fastest growing company at this year’s event.

"I was delighted to receive the notification from Ann Cox, the 2015 Georgia Fast 40 committee chair and a senior private banker for JP Morgan, acknowledging our recognition by the ACG,” said Jon Wright, CEO of APF. “To later be ranked the sixth fastest growing company during the ACG gala was truly the icing on the cake and represents the hard work our team has demonstrated during this post-recession era. I owe a great deal of gratitude to all of our partners that have displayed such passionate execution of our long-term, strategic plan and am humbled by this recognition."

Each year ACG Atlanta, the Atlanta Chapter of The Association for Corporate Growth® (ACG Atlanta), a global professional organization focused on middle-market growth, mergers and acquisitions, and private investment, honors the top 40 fastest growing middle-market companies in Georgia. Criteria and selection for the award includes the following:

Eligible companies must be for profit and headquartered in Georgia; Most recent year-end revenues ranging from $15 million to $500 million; and Three years of verifiable revenue and employment growth. “Access Point Financial exemplifies the type of company we should all be proud to have right in our back yard here in Atlanta,” said Melanie Brandt, ACG Atlanta Executive Director. “Access Point Financial weathered the storm of the economic downturn and has since experienced exceptional growth, continuing to serve its clients and better the hospitality industry while providing great jobs for many local, Atlanta residents.”

About Access Point Financial, Inc.

Based in Atlanta, Access Point Financial, Inc. (APF) is a direct full-service lender/servicer servicing the hospitality industry. APF was formed by a private equity firm based in Greenwich, Ct. and the APF Management team. APF lends to qualified franchisees of major hotel brands and independent boutique hotels throughout the United States and Canada and does not compete with its clients as owner operator of hotel assets. For more information, visit www.accesspointfinancial.com.

About ACG Atlanta

ACG Atlanta is the ultimate resource for professionals driving mergers and acquisitions. With more than 700 members and 25+ events per year, ACG brings together the dealmaking community, with more than 85 percent of its members reporting they have done business with fellow members. This results in real returns on investment for middle-market business leaders focused on building companies. Private equity professionals, investment bankers & intermediaries, attorneys, auditors & accountants, lenders, corporate development officers and company leaders make ACG the resource for opportunities, capital and services. To learn more visit http://www.acg.org/atlanta/.
