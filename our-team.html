OUR TEAM

Access Point Financial’s established team of hotel financing veterans has more than 25 years of experience in the hospitality and commercial real estate finance sector. Our team’s proven track record of sourcing, structuring, servicing and advising for our hospitality real estate clients, combined with our in-depth knowledge of the lodging industry, mortgage, bridge, FF&E and CapEx lending, enables us to quickly assess each opportunity with accuracy.

GIVING BACK

APF is a proud partner of the American Hotel & Lodging Educational Foundation (AH&LEF) Annual Giving Campaign. AH&LEF’s mission is to ensure talented hospitality management students get a strong start, today’s quality professionals stay in the industry and hotels are better prepared for tomorrow.

APF works closely with Cornell University as they cultivate the next generation of leaders within the hotel industry. APF through their Strategic Corporate Alliance with Cornell participates in roundtables with faculty and expert industry leaders as well as guest lectures for classes and external conferences.

APF continues to work with United Jewish Appeal (UJA) on their annual Hospitality Event honoring key executives in the hospitality lending industry.

In addition to supporting the hospitality industry, APF also works with the American Cancer Society and the Stone Point Charity Golf Tournament for Fibrolamellar Cancer Foundation to help with their dedication to the education and research about the causes, treatment and search for the cure to cancer.

APF also sponsors the Chick-fil-a Kickoff and Peach Bowl Games where they donate a number of tickets to the Boys & Girls Clubs of Metro Atlanta.
