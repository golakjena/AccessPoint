FINANCIAL PARTNERS

Access Point Financial, along with its private equity partner, Stone Point Capital, is backed by well-known banks such as Wells Fargo Capital Finance, Key Bank, EverBank Commercial Finance, East West Bank and CapitalSource, a division of Pacific Western Bank. Banks provide funding to APF because of our proven track record, hospitality franchise financing expertise, and our recognized ability to serve this market efficiently. Access Point Financial retains a significant equity interest in all loans it finances under its debt facilities.

Since inception in 2011, APF has surpassed the $500 million hotel loan threshold, representing 325 loans and more than $1 billion in hotel asset valuations.
